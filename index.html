About Nature

The natural beauty measure is the extent trees are present along the sidewalks. The more trees there are on a given block, the more sense of natural beauty it evokes.

About Comfort

Street furniture like benches for resting, trees for shading and sidewalk widths were used to determine comfort. Data available from open source include bus stop seats and park benches.

About Architecture

Architecture measures the display of iconic buildings. It also uses historic district data, building year, landmarks, plazas, churches, and open space.

About Interest

This walkability dimension means pedestrian space worth looking at. We extracted all points of interest in most tourist maps as well as cultural, educational and art destinations.

About Safety

Information on felony reports and accidents make up the rating of sidewalk safety. A high score is assigned to blocks with no traffic or crime incidents and a low score assigned to intersections with more than 50 felony reports or more than three accident reports.
